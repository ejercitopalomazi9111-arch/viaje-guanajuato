About the Project

An academic journey
as a formative experience.

This journey is not merely a recreational activity, but a structured formative experience that reinforces classroom knowledge through observation, analysis, and experimentation in real contexts.

Under STEAM methodology (Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, and Mathematics), it integrates seven academic subjects across eleven stops that account for 50% of the third term.
